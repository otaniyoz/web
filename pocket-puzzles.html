Pocket puzzles

Pocket puzzles is a collection of grid-based puzzles that are both simple and challenging by design:

There are three puzzles. In Slide, you slide the shuffled tiles into proper positions to restore the original image. In Pairs, you find pairs among the tiles in the grid. In That, you tap the tile that stands out from the rest before the timer runs out.

The main element of the game is a dynamic grid, where the number of rows and columns increases proportionally to difficulty:

At most, 32 distinct tiles are displayed at a given time. These tile patterns are selected from 42 unique designs:

Each design fits all puzzles: it can be restored if cut into 64 pieces and shuffled, and can be identified if scaled down. To fit the overall style, each tile pattern uses a limited pallete and basic shapes.

To provide feedback during the game, there are textured sound effects. They were created by manipulating heavy-weight paper and bubble wrap ASMR-close to the microphone. Then the sounds were chopped, filtered, and sped up; volume and sound duration was normalised. To reduce latency, the sound effects were implemented with the Web Audio API.
